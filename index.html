Our Mission

To plant 100 trees across Raleigh in 2015. To help the environment while increasing local food production and strengthen the community.

How it works

You contact us at FreeTreesRaleigh@gmail.com. We come and make sure you have a spot with plenty of sunshine and room to grow.

We plant the tree. All you have to do is water the tree if we don't have any rain for 10 days!
